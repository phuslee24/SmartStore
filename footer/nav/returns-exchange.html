RETURNS & EXCHANGE


Please note that sale items that are not defective are not eligible for exchanges or returns.


To be eligible for exchange, your exchange item/s must be:


  • Unworn
  • Unwashed
  • In its original sold condition with its tag intact

Products will be eligible for exchange within 30 days from the date of purchase. Please ensure that the item(s) are in original condition, original packaging and with the original tags attached. Returns that do not meet our policy will not be accepted and will be returned to you at your cost.


RETAIL EXCHANGE

You may exchange the items purchased online in any of our retail stores.


However, some of our stores do not carry all the styles showcased on our online store. For Size Exchanges, do contact us at hello@iora.com.sg to reserve a piece at your preferred location and we will make arrangement.


Once the exchange is arranged, please kindly bring along the item in its original condition with the original tag and packaging, as well as the printed invoice, to the indicated outlet in the email.


A final inspection will be done to ensure that all items are in their original condition before processing your returns. In the interest of hygiene, we do not accept returns or refunds on accessories unless they are defective or the wrong item was sent.


Please note that all exchanges are final, i.e. exchanged items cannot be further returned or exchanged.


To do an exchange, please email to hello@iora.online with the following:


Subject “(Order Number) | Exchange”

  • Order Number
  • Contact Number
  • Product name and Article Number
  • Attached image of defective or incorrect merchandise
  • Name of Product, Colour, Size, with what you would like to exchange with
  • Exchange Method: Retail (store location)

You will be contacted within 3 working days once the email is sent.

BOUNCED PARCELS

We’ll be in touch via email in the event of a bounced parcel. However, please note that we may charge you for further delivery costs.

Bounced parcels that are unclaimed after 1 year will be deemed as iORA’s property and no compensation will be offered as sufficient response time has been given.

TERMS & CONDITIONS FOR STORE CREDIT

iORA reserves all rights to inspect the condition of the merchandise before processing any returns or exchanges. Please note that we do not bear the shipping and handling charges of returning any rejected return or exchange request item/s to you. Refund will only be in form of store credits.


Should there be any dispute on the refund amount for any return or exchange, refund will be based on merchandise value on the invoice , after discount if applicable.


Our returns and exchanges policies do not cover damage resulting from unique, accidental, or random damage that is the result from use by you or wear and tear.
iORA ‘s decision on returns and exchanges are final.

  • Store credit is applicable only for online purchases at iora.online/sg
  • Store credit is valid for 3 months from the date of issue.
  • Store credit is not applicable for use in any of the iORA retail stores.
