PRIVACY POLICY




We are committed to safeguarding the privacy of all our shoppers. Your personal information may be shared with third party vendors with the sole purpose of order fulfilment. iORA will not disclose your personal information in any way without your permission. By placing an order with us, you agree and acknowledge our privacy policy.


Personal Information we collect include the following
1. Name
2. E-mail Address
3. Contact Number
4. Shipping Address
5. Payment information*


*Payment information are directly sent to payment gateway service provider and not stored anywhere on iORA’s database. If you have opted to remember payment information, the data is stored securely on Stripe’s database.

SECURITY

Shoppers are encouraged to set a strong password with a combination of alphabets, numbers, special characters and a minimum length of 8 characters.

By browsing our site, your browser will store cookies. If you do not wish to have cookies stored, you may change the settings on your browser anytime.
